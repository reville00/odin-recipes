Chickpea Tikka Masala

Description

My favorite cuisine is Indian and this dish hits the spot for my Indian cusine craving every time. Additionally it is very easy and straight forward. My kids love it and so does my wife. Try it with rice to mix things up.

This recipe is made with simple, wholesome ingredients. Take a trip to your local bulk food store and get nearly everything you need.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Onion
  • 5 cloves Garlic
  • Ginger (healthy amount)
  • Coconut Milk
  • Tomato Sauce
  • 2 cans chickpeas
  • Indian Spice Blend
  • Salt to taste
  • Noodles (or rice)

Steps

  1. Dice onion, garlic and ginger and sautee in skillet until onions are translucent.
  2. Add in tomato sauce, Coconut milk, chickpeas and spices. Bring to simmer.
  3. Cook noodles and strain.
  4. Sauce is done once onions are soft. Add in Spinach if desired and cook until soft.
  5. Serve sauce over noodles and enjoy.
