African Peanut Soup

Description

This is delicious, wholesome, creamy soup that is sure to satisfy your peanut butter craving. Serve this at a party and become an all time legend of the potluck. Low in sodium and rich in phytonutrients. This is a great dish to make and et on for days and days and it is bound to fill you up.

This recipe is made with simple, wholesome ingredients. Take a trip to your local bulk food store and get nearly everything you need.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Peanut Butter (natural preferred
  • 0.5 C Nutritional Yeast
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • 0.5 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 0.5 tsp Cumin
  • 0.5 tsp Salt
  • 0.25 tsp Turmeric
  • Kale
  • Red Cabbage
  • Bragg's Liquid Aminos
  • Juice of One Lemon
  • Sweet Onion

Steps

  1. Combine onion and diced tomatoes in food processor.
  2. Add to pot along with peanut butter, braggs, spices and nutritional yeast.
  3. Add kale and red cabbage and bring to simmer. Cook until all vegetables are soft.
