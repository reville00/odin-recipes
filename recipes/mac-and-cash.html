Mac and Cash

Description

This is the best vagan Mac and Cheese i have ever had. We call it Mac and Cash around my household. And it is seriously very easy to make. You can spruce it up with black beans, peas, spinach, brocolli or really whatever your heart may desire.

This recipe is made with simple, wholesome ingredients. Take a trip to your local bulk food store and get nearly everything you need.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 C Raw Cashews
  • Half Lemon
  • 0.5 C Nutritional Yeast
  • 1 C Water
  • 0.5 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 0.5 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 0.5 tsp Salt
  • 0.25 tsp Turmeric
  • 8oz. Noodles (I like Banza)

Steps

  1. Soak cashews in boiling water for at least 5 minutes.
  2. Add nutritional yeast, water, juice of lemon and all spices to blender
  3. Add Cashews (and spinach if you're privy) to blender and blend all ingerdients until smooth and creamy.
  4. Boil and strain noodles then remove from heat and stir in sauce. At this point you can add in black beans or peas or whatever else you may enjoy.
  5. Serve up and enjoy your meal!
